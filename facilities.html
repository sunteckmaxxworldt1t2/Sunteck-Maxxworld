Society's Facilities

At Sunteck Maxx World T1 & T2, we take immense pride in curating a lifestyle where convenience meets elegance, and every amenity echoes our commitment to community well-being and holistic living. Our residents enjoy a symphony of thoughtfully designed facilities — from the grandeur of the Auditorium Hall that hosts memorable gatherings and festive moments, to the energy of a modern Gymnasium where wellness takes center stage. The Kids Play-Zone sparkles with imagination and joy, while our Swimming Pool offers serenity in every splash. Architectural brilliance is showcased through our Expansive Facade, setting the tone for sophistication, while the Tranquil In-House Waterbody provides moments of calm reflection amidst the urban rhythm. For leisure and camaraderie, residents can engage in friendly competition at the Pool Table, sharpen their intellect over a game of Chess, or embrace movement and team spirit at the Badminton Court. Together, these facilities aren’t just amenities — they are pillars of an elevated community experience, nurturing wellness, bonding, and an inspired way of life in every corner.
